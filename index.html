Welcome to my professional portfolio.


Libraries balance two things. A library strives to serve those who need the most help in our society, and they organize information. Information is a word that is far more powerful than its common usage. Information can provide the ability to find a job, as well as support the ability to advance civilization. It can serve as a resource for feeding a family and filing taxes, as well as provide resources for the grand structure of scholarship and innovation. Bringing together these two concepts, serving communities and organizing information, is a passion for me. Finding technological solutions to the problems of everyday people makes me feel happy and fulfilled. One of my main future goals is to help librarians design technology, so that the most convenient functions and features can be created, rather than simply requested from outside sources.
