Welcome to my professional portfolio.


Libraries are the one place that balance the two things I feel the strongest about. They seek to serve those who need the most help in our society, and they organize information. Information is a word far more powerful than its common usage. Information is both the ability to find a job and the ability to advance civilization. It is both the resource for feeding a family and filing taxes, and the grand structure of scholarship and innovation. Bringing those two concepts together is a passion for me. Finding tech solutions to the problems of everyday people makes me happy and fulfilled. One of my main goals is to help librarians self-design a universal search catalog interface, so that the functions and features that are practical for individual libraries are also usable by those libraries, and not dependent on the whims of private firms.
